Rose Set

Oval Mats
MATERIALS: AMERICAN THREAD COMPANY The Famous "PURITAN" MERCERIZED CROCHET COTTON, Article 40 or "DE LUXE" Quality MERCERIZED CROCHET COTTON, Article 346 2 balls Shaded Yellows or color of your choice. 1 ball each Kelly Green and White will make the small mat. 1 additional ball Shaded Yellows is needed to make both mats.
Steel crochet hook No. 7.
SMALL MAT With Shaded Yellows ch 5, join to form a ring, ch 5, dc in ring, * ch 2, dc in ring, repeat from * 5 times, ch 2, join in 3rd st of ch.
2nd Row: Over each loop work 1 sc, 5 dc, 1 sc, join.
3rd Row: * Ch 5, sl st in back of work between next 2 petals, repeat from * 7 times.
4th Row: Over each loop work 1 sc, 7 dc, 1 sc, join.
5th Row: * Ch 6, sl st in back of work between next 2 petals, repeat from * all around, cut thread.
6th Row: Attach Green and work 1 sc, 1 dc, 7 trc, 1 dc, 1 sc over each loop, join, cut thread.
2nd FLOWER: Work 1st 5 rows same as 1st flower. Next Row. Attach Green and work 1 sc, 1 dc, 4 trc in same loop, join to center st of any Green petal of 1st flower ( to join: slip loop off hook, insert in center st of petal of 1st flower, pick up loop and pull through ), 3 trc, 1 dc, 1 sc in same space of 2nd flower, 1 sc, 1 dc, 4 trc in next loop of 2nd flower, join to center st of next petal of 1st flower, 3 trc, 1 dc, 1 sc in same space of 2nd flower, finish row same as 1st flower. Work 3rd flower in same manner, joining to 2 center petals of 2nd flower leaving 2 petals free at top and lower edge between joinings, cut thread.
Attach White between the 3rd and 4th free petal of end flower, ch 7, * sc in 2nd trc of next petal, ch 5, skip 3 trc, sc in next trc, ch 3, trc between petals, ch 3, repeat from * once, sc in 2nd trc of next petal, ch 5, skip 3 trc, sc in next trc, ch 3, trc between next 2 petals keeping last loop of trc on hook, trc in joining keeping last loop of trc on hook, trc between next 2 petals of next flower, keeping last loop of trc on hook, thread over and work off all loops at one time ( a trc cluster ), ch 3, sc in 2nd trc of next petal, ch 5, skip 3 trc, sc in next trc, ch 3, trc between petals, ch 3, sc in 2nd trc of next petal, ch 5, skip 3 trc, sc in next trc, ch 3, work a trc cluster between flowers, * ch 3, sc in 2nd trc of next petal, ch 5, skip 3 trc, sc in next trc, ch 3, trc between petals, repeat from * 4 times, ch 3, sc in 2nd trc of next petal, ch 5, skip 3 trc, sc in next trc, ch 3, trc cluster between flowers, finish row to correspond joining last ch 3 in 4th st of ch.
2nd Row: Sl st into loop, ch 6, dc in same space, * ch 1, 1 dc, ch 3, 1 dc ( shell) in next loop, repeat from * 6 times, ch 1, 1 trc, ch 3, 1 trc in trc cluster, skip the ch 3 loop, * ch 1, shell in next loop, repeat from * 3 times, ch 1, 1 trc, ch 3, 1 trc in next trc cluster, skip the ch 3 loop, * ch 1, shell in next loop, repeat from * 15 times, ch 1, 1 trc, ch 3, 1 trc in next trc cluster, skip the ch 3 loop, * ch 1, shell in next loop, repeat from * 3 times, ch 1, 1 trc, ch 3, 1 trc in next trc cluster, skip the ch 3 loop, * ch 1, shell in next loop, repeat from * 7 times, ch 1, join in 3rd st of ch.
3rd Row: Sl st into shell, ch 3, 1 dc, ch 2, 2 dc in same space, * 2 dc, ch 2, 2 dc ( shell) in next shell, repeat from * all around, join.
4th Row: Sl st to center of shell, * ch 7, sc in center of next shell, repeat from * all around, cut White.
5th Row: Attach Shaded Yellows in any loop, ch 3, 5 dc, ch 3, 6 dc over same loop, * 6 dc, ch 3, 6 dc over next loop, repeat from * all around, join.
6th Row: Ch 3, 1 dc in each dc and 2 dc, ch 3, 2 dc in each ch 3 loop, join.
7th Row: Ch 4, 1 trc in each dc and 2 trc, ch 3, 2 trc in each ch 3 loop, join, cut thread.
8th Row: Attach White in ch 3 loop, ch 3, 1 dc, ch 2, 2 dc in same space, * ch 1, 2 dc, ch 2, 2 dc ( shell) in next ch 3 loop, repeat from * all around, ch 1, join.
9th Row: Sl st to center of shell, ch 3, 2 dc, ch 2, 3 dc in same space, * ch 1, 3 dc, ch 2, 3 dc ( shell) in center of next shell, repeat from * all around, ch 1, join, cut thread.
10th Row: Attach Green in center of shell, ch 3, 2 dc, ch 2, 3 dc in same space, ch 1, sc in the ch 1 between shells, * ch 1, 3 dc, ch 2, 3 dc in center of next shell, ch 1, sc in the ch 1 between shells, repeat from * all around, ch 1, join, cut thread.
11th Row: Attach Shaded Yellows in any sc, * ch 1, 1 sc in each of the next 3 dc of next shell, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in center of same shell, 1 sc in each of the next 3 dc of same shell, ch 1, sc in next sc, repeat from * all around, join, cut thread.

The following directions are given if trivet cover is made.
CASING: With wrong side of work toward you, attach White between shells of 9th row, * ch 7, sc in the ch 1 between next 2 shells, repeat from * all around.
Next Row: Sl st to center of loop, * ch 7, sc in next loop, repeat from * all around.
Next Row: Same as last row but having 5 chs in each loop, cut thread.
LARGE MAT OR COVER Work flowers for center same as small mat but working 4 flowers instead of 3 and joining in same manner. Attach White and work in same manner as 1st row of small mat working over 3rd flower same as over 2nd flower and working a trc cluster between 3rd and 4th flowers.
2nd Row: Same as 2nd row of small mat but working shells across 3rd flower same as across 2nd flower and working 1 trc, ch 3, 1 trc in the trc cluster between the 3rd and 4th flowers.
3rd Row: Same as 3rd row of small mat.
4th and 5th Rows: Sl st to center of shell, ch 3, 1 dc, ch 2, 2 dc in same space, * 2 dc, ch 2, 2 dc ( shell) in center of next shell, repeat from * all around, join. Repeat the 4th, 5th, 6th, 7th, 8th and 9th rows of small mat.
12th Row: Same as last row, cut White.
Next Row: Attach Green and work same as 10th row of small mat.
Next Row: Attach Shaded Yellows and work same as 11th row of small mat.
CASING: Work same as for small cover attaching thread in 10th row, but having 3 rows of 7 ch loops instead of 2 rows.

Napkin Holder Cover
Materials Required: AMERICAN THREAD COMPANY The Famous "PURITAN" MERCERIZED CROCHET COTTON, Article 40 or "DE LUXE" Quality MERCERIZED CROCHET COTTON, Article 346 1 ball each White, Shaded Yellows and Kelly Green or colors of your choice.
Steel crochet hook No. 6 or No. 7. 1 Napkin Holder 5 inches in diameter.
Center: With Shaded Yellows ch 5, join to form a ring, ch 5, dc in ring, * ch 2, dc in ring, repeat from * 5 times, ch 2, join in 3rd st of ch.
2nd Round: Over each loop work 1 sc, 5 dc, 1 sc, join.
3rd Round: * Ch 5, sl st in back of work between next 2 petals, repeat from * 7 times.
4th Round: Over each loop work 1 sc, 7 dc, 1 sc, join.
5th Round: * Ch 6, sl st in back of work between next 2 petals, repeat from * all around, cut thread.
6th Round: Attach Green and work 1 sc, 1 dc, 7 trc, 1 dc, 1 sc over each loop, join, cut thread.
7th Round: Attach White in 2nd trc of Green petal, ch 5, skip 3 trc, sc in next trc, * ch 3, trc between petals, ch 3, sc in 2nd trc of next Green petal, ch 5, skip 3 trc, sc in next trc, repeat from * all around ending with ch 3, trc between petals, ch 3, join.
8th Round: Sl st into loop, ch 6, dc in same loop, * 1 dc, ch 3, 1 dc in next loop, repeat from * all around, join in 3rd st of ch.
9th Round: Sl st to center of next loop, * ch 6, sc in next loop, repeat from * all around ending with ch 6, join in sl st, cut thread.
10th Round: Attach Shaded Yellows in loop, ch 3, 4 dc in same loop, ch 3, 5 dc in same loop, * 5 dc, ch 3, 5 dc in next loop, repeat from * all around, join.
11th Round: Ch 3, work 1 dc in each dc and 2 dc, ch 3, 2 dc in each ch 3 loop, join, cut thread.
12th Round: Attach White in ch 3 at point, ch 3, 1 dc, ch 2, 2 dc in same space, * ch 1, 2 dc, ch 2, 2 dc ( shell) in next ch 3 loop, repeat from * all around, ch 1, join, cut thread.
13th Round: Attach Green in center of shell, ch 3, 2 dc, ch 2, 3 dc in same space, ch 1, sc in ch 1 between shells, * ch 1, 3 dc, ch 2, 3 dc in center of next shell, ch 1, sc in ch 1 between shells, repeat from * all around, ch 1, join, cut thread.
14th Round: Attach Shaded Yellows in any sc, * ch 1, 1 sc in each of the next 3 dc of next shell, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in center of same shell, 1 sc in each of the next 3 dc of same shell, ch 1, sc in next sc, repeat from * all around, join, cut thread.
CASING: With wrong side of work toward you, attach White between shells of 12th round, * ch 7, sc in ch 1 between next 2 shells of same round, repeat from * to within the last 6 shells, turn.
Next Row Sl st to center of loop, * ch 7, sc in next loop, repeat from * to same space as opposite side, turn.
Next Row: Same as last row but having 6 chs in each loop, turn.
Next Row: Same as last row but having 4 chs in each loop, cut thread. Work another section in same manner.

Rose Round Mat Cover
Materials Required: AMERICAN THREAD COMPANY The Famous "PURITAN" MERCERIZED CROCHET COTTON, Article 40 or "DE LUXE" Quality MERCERIZED CROCHET COTTON, Article 346 1 ball each White, Kelly Green and Shaded Yellows or colors of your choice.
Steel crochet hook No. 7.

With Shaded Yellows ch 7, join to form a ring, ch 7, dc in ring, * ch 3, dc in ring, repeat from * 5 times, ch 3, join in 3rd st of ch 7.
2nd Round: Ch 1 and over each loop work 1 sc, 1 dc, 3 trc, 1 dc, 1 sc, join.
3rd Round: Sl st in back of petal, * ch 5, sc between next 2 petals in back of work, repeat from * 7 times.
4th Round: Ch 1 and over each loop work 1 sc, 1 dc, 5 trc, 1 dc, 1 sc, join.
5th Round: Slip st in back of petal, * ch 7, sc between next 2 petals in back of work, repeat from * 7 times, cut thread.
6th Round: Attach Green, ch 1 and over each loop work 1 sc, 1 dc, 7 trc, 1 dc, 1 sc, join, cut thread.
7th Round: Attach White in 2nd trc of Green petal, ch 7, skip 3 trc, sc in next trc, * ch 5, trc between petals, ch 5, sc in 2nd trc of next petal, ch 7, skip 3 trc, sc in next trc, repeat from * 6 times, ch 5, trc between petals, ch 5, join.
8th Round: Sl st to loop, ch 3, dc in same space, ch 3, 2 dc in same space, 2 dc in next loop, ch 3, 2 dc in same loop ( shell, ) work a shell in each loop, join in 3rd st of ch 3.
9th Round: Sl st to center of shell, ch 3, dc in same space, ch 3, 2 dc in same space, ch 1 and work a shell in center of each shell with ch 1 between shells, join.
10th Round: Sl st to center of shell, * ch 8, sc in center of next shell, repeat from * all around, cut thread.
11th Round: Attach Shaded Yellows in loop, ch 3, 5 dc, ch 3, 6 dc in same loop, * 6 dc, ch 3, 6 dc in next loop, repeat from * all around, join.
12th Round: Ch 3 and work 1 dc in each dc and 2 dc, ch 3, 2 dc in each ch 3 loop, join.
13th Round: Ch 4, and work 1 trc in each dc and 2 trc, ch 3, 2 trc in each ch 3 loop, join, cut thread.
14th Round: Attach White in ch 3 loop at point, ch 3 and work a shell in same space, * ch 4, work a shell in next ch 3 loop, repeat from * all around, ch 4, join.
15th Round: Sl st to center of shell, ch 3 and work a shell in same space, * ch 5, shell in next shell, repeat from * all around, ch 5, join. Work 1 more row of shells with ch 6 between shells.
17th Round: Slip st to center of shell, ch 3, 2 dc in same space, ch 3, 3 dc in same space, * ch 3, sc in next loop, ch 3, 3 dc, ch 3, 3 dc in next shell, repeat from * all around ending with ch 3, sc in next loop, ch 3, join, cut thread.
18th Round: Attach Green in center of shell, ch 3, 2 dc, ch 3, 3 dc in same space, * ch 3, sc in next sc, ch 3, 3 dc, ch 3, 3 dc in center of next shell, repeat from * all around ending with ch 3, sc in next sc, ch 3, join, cut thread.
19th Round: Attach Shaded Yellows in any sc, * ch 1, 2 sc in next loop, 1 sc in each of the next 3 dc of next shell, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in center of same shell, 1 sc in each of the next 3 dc of same shell, 2 sc in next loop, ch 1, sc in next sc, repeat from * all around, join, cut thread.
20th Round:
CASING: With wrong side of work toward you join White in loop of 16th round, * ch 10, sc over next loop of same round, repeat from * all around.
21st Round: Sl st to center of next loop and work a row of ch 9 loops, join in last sl st.
22nd Round: Same as last round, cut thread.
