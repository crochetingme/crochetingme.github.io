Round And Round Tablecloth Pattern

MATERIALS:
AMERICAN THREAD The Famous "PURITAN" MERCERIZED CROCHET COTTON 22-250 yd. Balls White
or
"GIANT" MERCERIZED CROCHET COTTON 10-550 yd. Balls White
Steel Crochet Hook No. 7
1st Rnd: Ch 4, join to form a ring, ch 1, work 8 sc in ring, join in 1st sc.
2nd Rnd: Ch 3 (counts as 1st dc), dc in same space, * ch 3, 2 dc in next sc, repeat from * 6 times, ch 3, join in top of ch 3 at beg.
3rd Rnd: Sl st into ch 3 loop, ch 1, sc in same loop, *ch 5, sc in next loop, repeat from * 6 times, ch 5, join in 1st sc.
4th Rnd: Sl st into loop, ch 1, sc in same loop, s dc in same loop (s dc: thread over hook, insert in loop, pull through, thread over and work off all 3 loops at one time), then 3 dc, s dc, sc in same loop, * l sc, l sdc, 3 dc, l sdc, 1 sc in next loop, repeat from * all around, join in 1st sc.
5th Rnd: Sl st to 1st dc, ch 3, sl st in same space for picot, * ch 7, skip next dc, sl st in next dc, ch 3, sl st in same space for picot, ch 7, skip next 4 sts, sl st in next dc, ch 3, sl st in same space, repeat from * 6 times, ch 7, skip next dc, picot in next dc, ch 3, tr c in same space where 1st picot was worked (this brings thread in position for next round).
6th and 7th Rnds: Picot in top of tr c, * ch 7, picot in center st of next loop, repeat from * all around, ending with ch 3, tr c in tr c.
8th Rnd: * Ch 11, sc in next loop, repeat from * all around, ending with ch 11, sl st in tr c.
9th Rnd: Sl st into loop, ch 1, 6 sc, ch 2, 6 sc in same loop, * 6 sc, ch 2, 6 sc in next loop, repeat from * all around, join in 1st sc.
10th Rnd: Sl st to ch 2 loop, * ch 11, sc in next ch 2 loop, repeat from * all around, ch 11, join.
11th Rnd: Ch 1, 13 sc in next loop, * ch 5, cluster st in center st of next loop (cluster st: keeping last loop of each st on hook, 3 tr c in same st, thread over and work off all loops at one time), ch 3, cluster st, ch 3, cluster st in same space, ch 5, 13 sc in next loop, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join in 1st sc.
12th Rnd: Ch 1, * 1 sc in each of the next 11 sc, 7 sc in next loop, 5 sc in next loop, ch 2, 5 sc in next loop, 7 sc in next loop, skip next sc of next sc group, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join in 1st sc.
13th Rnd: Sl st to center sc, ch 4, keeping last loop of each st on hook, 2 tr c in same space, thread over and work off all loops at one time, * ch 11, sl st in center sc of next 5 sc group, ch 3, sl st in next ch 2 loop, ch 3, sl st in center st of next 5 sc group, ch 11, cluster st in center sc of next 11 sc group, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join in 1st cluster st.
14th Rnd: * 6 sc, ch 2, 6 sc in next loop, ch 5, 3 sc in next loop, 6 sc, ch 2, 6 sc in next loop, picot in next cluster st, repeat from * all around, join.
15th Rnd: Sl st to ch 2 loop, ch 1, sc in same loop, ** ch 11, sc in next ch 5 loop, * ch 11, sc in next ch 2 loop, repeat from * once, repeat from ** all around, ending with ch 5, tr tr c (4 times over hook) in 1st sc.
16th Rnd: Ch 3, dc over tr tr c, * ch 11, 2 dc in center st of next loop, repeat from * all around, ending with ch 11, join in 3rd st of ch.
17th Rnd: Sl st to center st of next loop, ch 3, dc in same space, * ch 11, 2 dc in center st of next loop, repeat from * all around, ending with ch 11, join in 3rd st of ch.
18th Rnd: Sl st into loop, ch 1, work 1 sc, 1 s dc, 9 dc, 1 s dc, 1 sc in each loop, join in 1st sc.
19th Rnd: Sl st to 1st dc, picot in same space, * ch 7, skip next 3 dc, picot in next dc, ch 7, skip next 3 dc, picot in next dc, ch 7, skip next 4 sts, picot in next st, repeat from * all around, ending last repeat with ch 3, tr c in same space as 1st picot.
20th Rnd: Picot in same space, * ch 7, picot in center st of next loop, repeat from * all around, ending with ch 3, tr c in tr c.
21st to 37th Rnds: Repeat last round.
38th to 44th Rnds: Repeat 8th to 14th rounds.
45th Rnd: Work same as 15th round but work ch 9 instead of ch 11, and end with ch 4, tr c in 1st sc.
46th Rnd: Work same as 16th round but ch 9 instead of ch 11.
47th Rnd: Work same as 17th round but ch 9.
48th to 51st Rnds: Repeat last round.
52nd to 60th Rnds: Same as 17th round.
61st Rnd: Same as 18th round.
62nd Rnd: Same as 19th round.
63rd to 85th Rnds: Same as 20th round.
86th to 97th Rnds: Same as 20th round but ch 9, and end with ch 4, d tr c (3 times over hook) in tr c.
98th to 118th Rnds: Same as 20th round but ch 11, and end with ch 4, dtrc in d tr c.
119th Rnd: Repeat 8th round.
120th Rnd: Repeat 9th round.
121st Rnd: Repeat 10th round.
122nd Rnd: Repeat 11th round.
123rd Rnd: Repeat 12th round.
124th Rnd: Repeat 13th round.
125th Rnd: * 6 sc, ch 5 picot, 5 sc in next loop, 3 sc in next loop, sl st in next sl st between 2 ch 3 loops, ch 5 picot in same space, 3 sc in next loop, 6 sc, ch 5 picot, 5 sc in next loop, ch 5 picot in next cluster st, repeat from * all around, join in 1st sc, cut thread.
FINISHING: Pin and block to size. If necessary divide into 8 sections, and block one section at a time.
