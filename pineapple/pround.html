Pineapple Round Doily Pattern
MATERIALS:
AMERICAN THREAD COMPANY
The Famous "PURITAN" MERCERIZED CROCHET COTTON, Article 40 2 balls White
or
The Famous "PURITAN" STAR SPANGLED MERCERIZED CROCHET COTTON, Article 40 3 balls Silver Spangle or color of your choice
Steel Crochet Hook No. 7 App. size: 17 inches in diameter for "PURITAN"; 19 inches in diameter for "PURITAN" STAR SPANGLED
1st Rnd: Chain (ch) 4, double crochet (dc) in 1st stitch (st) of ch, * ch 3, 2 dc in same space, repeat from * 6 times, ch 3, join in 4th st of ch.
2nd Rnd: Slip stitch (sl st) to next loop, ch 3, 1 dc, ch 3, 2 dc in same space, * 2 dc, ch 3, 2 dc in next loop, repeat from * 6 times, join.
3rd Rnd: Sl st to center st of next loop, ch 7, cluster over next 4 dc (cluster: keeping last loop of each st on hook work I dc in each st, thread over and work off all loops at one time), * ch 4, dc in next loop, ch 4, cluster over next 4 dc, repeat from * all around, ch 4, join in 3rd st of ch.
4th Rnd: Ch 1, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in same space, * ch 4, dc in next cluster, ch 4, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in next dc, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join in 1st sc.
5th Rnd: Sl st into loop, ch 4, treble crochet (trc) in same space, ch 3, 2 trc, ch 3, 2 trc in same space, * ch 1, sc in next dc, ch 1, skip next loop, 2 trc, ch 3, 2 trc, ch 3, 2 trc in next loop, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join.
6th Rnd: Ch 4, trc in next trc, * ch 7, keeping last loop of each st on hook work 2 trc in each of the next 2 loops, thread over and work off all loops at one time, ch 7, trc cluster over next 2 trc (trc cluster: keeping last loop of each st on hook work 1 trc in each st, thread over and work off all loops at one time), ch 3, cluster over next 2 trc, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join with a dc in 1st trc.
7th Rnd: Ch 1, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc over the dc, * ch 9, sc in next loop, ch 9, sc in next loop, ch 9, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in next loop, repeat from * all around ending with ch. 4, double treble crochet (d trc: 3 times over hook) in 1st sc (this brings thread in position for next round).
8th Rnd: Ch 3, sc over d trc, * ch 7, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in next large loop, repeat from * all around ending with ch 3, trc in d trc.
9th Rnd: Ch 3, sc over trc, * ch 8, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in next large loop, repeat from * all around ending with ch 4, trc in trc.
10th Rnd: Ch 3, sc over trc, * ch 9, 5 sc over last half of next loop, 1 sc, ch 5, 1 sc in next small loop, 5 sc over first half of next loop, ch 9, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in next large loop, repeat from * all around ending with ch 4, d trc in trc.
11th Rnd: 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc over d trc, * ch 9, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in next large loop, ch 4, 9 trc in next ch 5 loop, ch 4, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in next large loop, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join in d trc.
12th Rnd: Sl st to center st of next ch 9 loop, ch 3, 1 dc, ch 3, 2 dc, ch 3, 2 dc in same st, * ch 6, sc in next trc, * ch 3, sc in next trc, repeat from * 7 times, ch 6, skip next 2 loops, 2 dc, ch 3, 2 dc, ch 3, 2 dc in center st of next ch 9 loop, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join in 3rd st of ch.
13th Rnd: Sl st to next loop, ch 3, 1 dc, ch 3, 2 dc in same loop, ** 2 dc, ch 3, 2 dc in next loop (shell), ch 5, skip next loop, sc in 1st loop of pineapple, * ch 3, sc in next 1oop, repeat from * 6 times, ch 5, skip next loop, shell in next loop, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join in 3rd st of ch.
14th Rnd: Sl st to next loop, ch 3, 1 dc, ch 3, 2 dc in same loop, ** ch 4, dc cluster over next 4 dc, ch 4, shell in next shell, ch 4, skip next loop, sc in 1st loop of pineapple, * ch 3, sc in next loop, repeat from * 5 times, ch 4, shell in next shell, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, join.
15th Rnd: Sl st to next loop, ch 3, 1 dc, ch 3, 2 dc in same loop, ** ch 7, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in next cluster st, ch 7, shell in next shell, ch 3, skip next loop, sc in next loop, * ch 3, sc in next loop, repeat from * 4 times, ch 3, shell in next shell, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, join.
16th Rnd: Sl st to next loop, ch 3, 1 dc, ch 3, 2 dc in same loop, ** ch 7, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in next loop, ch 7, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in next ch 7 loop, ch 7, shell in next shell, ch 3, skip next loop, sc in next loop, * ch 3, sc in next loop, repeat from * 3 limes, ch 3, shell in next shell, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, join.
17th Rnd: Sl st to loop, ch 3, 1 dc, ch 3, 2 dc in same loop, ** ch 7, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in next ch 7 loop, repeat from * twice, ch 7, shell in next shell, ch 3, skip next loop, sc in next loop, * ch 3, sc in next loop, repeat from * twice, ch 3, shell in next shell, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond join.
18th Rnd: Sl st to next loop, ch 3, 1 dc, ch 3, 2 dc in same loop, * ch 7, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in next loop, ch 5, sc in next ch 7 loop, 7 trc in next ch 3 loop, sc in next loop, ch 5, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in next ch 7 loop, ch 7, shell in next shell, ch 3, skip next loop, sc in next loop, ch 3, sc in next loop, ch 3, sc in next loop, ch 3, shell in next shell, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join.
19th Rnd: Sl st to loop, ch 3, 1 dc, ch 3, 2 dc in same loop, ** ch 7, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in next loop, ch 3, shell in next loop, ch 3, sc in next trc, * ch 3, sc in next trc, repeat from * 5 times, ch 3, shell in next shell, ch 3, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in next loop, ch 7, shell in next shell, ch 3, skip next loop, sc in next loop, ch 3, sc in next loop, ch 3, shell in next shell, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond,join in 3rd st of ch.
20th Rnd: Sl st to center of next ch 7 loop, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in same loop, ** ch 7, skip next 2 loops, shell in next shell, ch 3, skip next loop, sc in next loop, * ch 3, sc in next loop, repeat from * 4 times, ch 3, skip next loop, shell in next loop, ch 7, skip next 2 loops, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in next loop, ch 11, sl st in next loop, skip the next 3 loops, sl st in next loop, ch 3, sl st in 4th st from end of ch 11 loop, ch 4, sl st in same space for picot, ch 7, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in next loop, repeat from ** 6 times, ch 7, skip next 2 loops, shell in next loop, ch 3, skip next loop, sc in next loop, * ch 3, sc in next loop, repeat from * 4 times, ch 3, shell in next shell, ch 7, skip next 2 loops, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in next loop, ch 11, sl st in next loop, skip next 3 loops, sl st in next loop, sl st in 4th st from end of ch 11 loop, ch 4, sl st in same space for picot, ch 4, trc in 1st sc.
21st Rnd: ** Ch 7, sc in next ch 7 loop, ch 7, shell in next shell, ch 3, skip next loop, sc in next loop, * ch 3, sc in next loop, repeat from * 3 times, ch 3, skip next loop, shell in next loop, ch 7, sc in next loop, ch 7, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in next ch 7 loop, ch 3, 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in next ch 7 loop, repeat from ** all around ending the last repeat with 1 sc, ch 3, 1 sc in the trc.
22nd Rnd: Sl st to center of next loop, ** ch 7, sc in next loop, ch 7, shell in next shell, ch 3, skip next loop, sc in next loop, * ch 3, sc in next loop, repeat from * twice, ch 3, shell in next shell, ch 7, sc in next loop, ch 7, sc in next loop, ch 9, sc in next ch 7 loop, repeat from ** all around ending with ch 4, d trc in last sl st.
23rd Rnd: Ch 4, trc in same space, * ch 3, 2 trc, ch 3, 2 trc in same space, ch 3, sc in next loop, ch 7, sc in next loop, ch 7, shell in next shell, ch 3, skip next loop, sc in next loop, ch 3, sc in next loop, ch 3, sc in next loop, ch 3, shell in next shell, ch 7, sc in next loop, ch 7, sc in next loop, ch 3, 2 trc in center st of next loop, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join in 4th st of ch.
24th Rnd: Ch 4, trc in next trc, ch 4, sl st in top of trc for picot, * ch 7, keeping last loop of each st on hook work 2 trc in each of the next 2 loops, thread over and work off all loops at one time, ch 4, sl st in top of cluster just made for picot, ch 7, trc cluster st over the next 2 trc, ch 4, sl st in top of cluster st for picot, ch 3, sc in next large loop, ch 7, sc in next loop, ch 7, shell in next shell, ch 3, skip next loop, sc in next loop, ch 3, sc in next loop, ch 3, shell in next shell, ch 7, sc in next loop, ch 7, sc in next loop, ch 3, trc cluster st over next 2 trc, ch 4, sl st in top of cluster st for picot, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, join in 1st cluster st.
25th Rnd: Sl st to center of next loop, * ch 11, sc in next loop, ch 9, skip next loop, sc in next loop, ch 7, sc in next loop, ch 7, shell in next shell, skip 1 loop, trc in next loop, shell in next shell, ch 7, sc in next loop, ch 7, sc in next loop, ch 9, skip next loop, sc in next loop, repeat from * all around ending last repeat with ch 4, d trc in last sl st.
26th Rnd: Ch 4, dc in d trc, ch 5, sl st in 4th st from hook for picot, ch 1, 2 dc in same d trc, ch 4, sl st in base of d trc, ** ch 5, 2 dc in center st of next loop, ch 5, sl st in 4th st from hook for picot, ch 1, 2 dc in same st of loop, ch 5, sl st in next sc, ch 4, 2 dc in center st of next loop, ch 5, sl st in 4th st from hook for picot, ch 1, 2 dc in same st of loop, ch 4, sl st in next sc, * 3 sc, ch 3, 3 sc in next loop, repeat from * once, sl st in next dc, ch 3, keeping last loop of each st on hook work 2 dc in loop of each of the next 2 shells, thread over and work off all loops at one time, ch 4, sl st in top of st just mad ch 3, skip next dc, sl st in next dc, * 3 sc, ch 3, 3 sc in next loop, repeat from * once, sl st in next sc, ch 4, 2 dc in center st of next loop, ch 5, sl st in 4th st from hook for picot, ch 1, 2 dc in same st of loop, ch 4, sl st in next sc, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, join, cut thread.
