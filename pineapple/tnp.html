The New Pineapple Doily Pattern

MATERIALS:
AMERICAN THREAD COMPANY "GEM" MERCERIZED CROCHET COTTON, Article 35, size 30 2 balls Buttercup or color of your choice.
Steel crochet hook No. 12.
Approximate size: 17 inches in diameter or The Famous "PURITAN"
MERCERIZED CROCHET COTTON, Article 40 or "DE LUXE" Quality
MERCERIZED CROCHET COTTON, Article 346 5 balls Lt. Yellow or color of your choice.
Steel crochet hook No. 7.
Approximate size: 24 inches in diameter.
Chain (ch) 8, join to form a ring, ch 3, counts as 1 double crochet (dc), 19 more dc in ring, join in 3rd stitch (st) of ch.
2nd Rnd: Ch 7, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc, * ch 4, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc, repeat from * all around, ch 4, join in 3rd st of ch.
3rd Rnd: Ch 3, * 5 dc in next loop, dc in next dc, repeat from * all around ending with 5 dc in last loop, join in 3rd st of ch.
4th Rnd: Ch 3 and working in dc, increase 4 dc evenly spaced, join (64 dc).
5th Rnd: Ch 3, 2 dc in same space, ch 3, 3 dc in same space, * ch 6, skip 7 dc, 5 treble crochet (trc) in next dc, ch 6, skip 7 dc, 3 dc, ch 3, 3 dc (shell) in next dc, repeat from * all around ending with ch 6, skip 7 dc, 5 trc in next dc, ch 6, join.
6th Rnd: Slip stitch (sl st) to center of shell, ch 3 (always counts as part of 1st shell in each round), shell in same space, * ch 6, 2 trc in each of the next 5 trc, ch 6, shell in next shell, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, ch 6, join.
7th Rnd: Sl st to center of shell, ch 3, shell in same space, ** ch 6, * 2 trc in next trc, trc in next trc, repeat from * once, 2 trc in next trc, ch 1, 2 trc in next trc, * trc in next trc, 2 trc in next trc, repeat from * once, ch 6, shell in next shell, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, ch 6, join.
8th Rnd: Sl st to center of shell, ch 3, shell in same space, * ch 5, skip 1 trc, 2 dc, ch 2, 2 dc in next trc, ch 5, skip 6 trc, 3 dc, ch 3, 3 dc (shell) in next ch 1 space, ch 5, skip 6 trc, 2 dc, ch 2, 2 dc in next trc, ch 5, shell in next shell, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, ch 5, join.
9th Rnd: Sl st to center of shell, ch 3, shell in same space, * ch 4, dc over end of next loop, 1 dc in each of the next 2 dc, ch 3, 1 dc in each of the next 2 dc, dc over beginning of next loop, ch 4, shell in next shell, ch 4, dc over end of next loop, 1 dc in each of the next 2 dc, ch 3, 1 dc in each of the next 2 dc, dc over beginning of next loop, ch 4, shell in next shell, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, ch 4, join.
10th Rnd: Sl st to center of shell, ch 3, shell in same space, * ch 4, dc over end of next loop, 1 dc in each of the next 3 dc, dc in next loop, ch 3, dc in same space, 1 dc in each of the next 3 dc, dc over beginning of next loop, ch 4, shell in next shell, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, ch 4, join.
11th Rnd: Sl st to center of shell, ch 3, 2 dc, ch 3, 3 dc, ch 3, 3 dc in same space, * ch 2, dc over end of next loop, 1 dc in each of the next 5 dc, dc in next loop, ch 5, dc in same space, 1 dc in each of the next 5 dc, dc over beginning of next loop, ch 2, 3 dc, ch 3, 3 dc, ch 3, 3 dc in center of next shell, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, ch 2, join.
12th Rnd: Sl st to next loop, ch 3, shell in same space, shell in next loop, * ch 2, skip 1 dc, 1 dc in each of the next 5 dc, ch 2, 7 trc in center of the next loop, ch 2, skip 1 dc, 1 dc in each of the next 5 dc, ch 2, skip the ch 2 loop, shell in next loop, shell in next loop, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, ch 2, join.
13th Rnd: Sl st into shell, ch 3, shell in same space. * ch 1, shell in next shell, ch 2, skip 1 dc, 1 dc in each of the next 3 dc, ch 2, 1 trc in each of the next 7 trc with ch 1 between each trc, ch 2, skip 1 dc, 1 dc in each of the next 3 dc, ch 2, shell in next shell, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, ch 2, join.
14th Rnd: Sl st to center of shell, ch 3, shell in same space, ** ch 2, shell in next shell, ch 1, 1 dc in each of the next 2 dc, ch 2, single crochet (sc) between 1st 2 trc, * ch 3, sc in next ch 1 space, repeat from * 4 times, ch 2, skip 1 dc, 1 dc in each of the next 2 dc, ch 1, shell in next shell, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, ch 1, join.
15th Rnd: Sl st to center of shell, ch 3, shell in same space, ** ch 4, shell in next shell, ch 1, dc in next dc, ch 3, skip 1 loop, sc in 1st loop of pineapple, * ch 3, sc in next loop, repeat from * 3 times, ch 3, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc, ch 1, shell in next shell, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, ch 1, join.
16th Rnd: Sl st to center of shell, ch 3, shell in same space, ** ch 5, sc in next loop, ch 5, shell in next shell, ch 5, skip 2 loops, sc in 1st loop of pineapple, * ch 3, sc in next loop, repeat from * twice, ch 5, skip 2 loops, shell in next shell, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, ch 5, join.
17th Rnd: Sl st into shell, ch 3, shell in same space, * ch 5, sc in next loop, ch 5, sc in next loop, ch 5, shell in next shell, ch 5, skip 1 loop, sc in 1st loop of pineapple, ch 3, sc in next loop, ch 3, sc in next loop, ch 5, shell in next shell, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, ch 5, join.
18th Rnd: Sl st into shell, ch 3, shell in same space, * ch 5, sc in next loop, repeat from * twice, ch 5, shell in next shell, ch 5, sc in 1st loop of pineapple, ch 3, sc in next loop, ch 5, shell in next shell, repeat from 1st * all around ending to correspond, ch 5, join.
19th Rnd: Sl st to center of shell, ch 3, shell in same space, * ch 5, sc in next loop, repeat from * 3 times, ch 5, shell in next shell, ch 5, sc in remaining loop of pineapple, ch 5, shell in next shell, repeat from 1st * all around ending to correspond, ch 5, join.
20th Rnd: Sl st to center of shell, ch 3, shell in same space, * ch 6, sc in next loop, repeat from * 4 times, ch 6, shell in next shell, ch 5, sc in sc at top of pineapple, ch 5, shell in next shell, repeat from 1st * all around ending to correspond, ch 5, join.
21st Rnd: Sl st to center of shell, ch 3, shell in same space, * ch 7, sc in next loop, repeat from * 5 times, ch 7, shell in next shell, shell in next shell, repeat from 1st * all around ending to correspond, join.
22nd Rnd: Sl st to center of shell, * ch 7, sc in next loop, repeat from * 6 times, ch 7, sc in center of next shell, ch 7. sc in center of next shell, repeat from 1st * all around ending last repeat with ch 3, trc in last sl st.
23rd Rnd: Ch 7, sc in next loop, repeat from * all around ending with ch 3, trc in trc.
24th, 25th and 26th Rnds: * Ch 8, sc in next loop, repeat from * all around ending each round with ch 4, trc in trc.
27th Rnd: * Ch 9, sc in next loop, repeat from * all around ending with ch 4, double treble crochet [ (d trc) 3 times over hook] in trc.
28th Rnd: Same as last round but ending with ch 9, sl st in dtrc.
29th Rnd: Sl st to center st of next loop, ch 3, 2 dc, ch 3, 3 dc in same space, * ch 5, sc in next loop, ch 5, 2 dc, ch 2, 2 dc in center st of next loop, ch 5, sc in next loop, ch 5, 3 dc, ch 3, 3 dc (shell) in center st of next loop, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, ch 5, join.
30th Rnd: Sl st to center of shell, ch 3, shell in same space, * ch 8, skip 1 loop, dc over end of next loop, 1 dc in each of the next 2 dc, ch 4, 1 dc in each of the next 2 dc, dc over beginning of next loop, ch 8, shell in next shell, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, ch 8, join.
31st Rnd: Sl st to center of shell, ch 3, shell in same space, * ch,8, dc over end of next loop, 1 dc in each of the next 3 dc, dc over beginning of next loop, ch 4, dc in same space, 1 dc in each of the next 3 dc, dc over beginning of next loop, ch 8, shell in next shell, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, ch 8, join.
32nd Rnd: Sl st to center of shell, ch 3, shell in same space, * ch 7, dc over end of next loop, 1 dc in each of the next 5 dc, dc over beginning of next loop, ch 4, dc in same space, 1 dc in each of the next 5 dc, dc over beginning of next loop, ch 7, shell in next shell, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, ch 7, join.
33rd Rnd: Sl st to center of shell, ch 3, 2 dc, ch 3, 3 dc, ch 3, 3 dc in same space, * ch 6, skip 1 dc, 1 dc in each of the next 5 dc, ch 2, 9 trc in next loop, ch 2, skip 1 dc, 1 dc in each of the next 5 dc, ch 6, 3 dc, ch 3, 3 dc, ch 3, 3 dc in center of next shell, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, ch 6, join.
34th Rnd: Sl st to loop of shell, ch 3, shell in same space, * shell in next ch 3 loop, ch 4, 1 dc in each of the next 4 dc, ch 2, 1 trc in each of the next 9 trc with ch 1 between each trc, ch 2, skip 1 dc, 1 dc in each of the next 4 dc, ch 4, shell in next ch 3 loop, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, ch 4, join.
35th Rnd: Sl st into shell, ch 3, shell in same space, ** ch 3, shell in next shell, ch 2, 1 dc in each of the next 3 dc, ch 3, sc between 1st 2 trc, * ch 3, sc in next ch 1 space, repeat from * 6 times, ch 3, skip 1 dc, 1 dc in each of the next 3 dc, ch 2, shell in next shell, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, ch 2, join.
36th Rnd: Sl st to center of shell, ch 3, shell in same space, * ch 3, sc in next loop, ch 3, shell in next shell, ch 1, 1 dc in each of the next 2 dc, ch 3, skip 1 loop, sc in 1st loop of pineapple, * ch 3, sc in next loop, repeat from * 5 times, ch 3, skip 1 dc, 1 dc in each of the next 2 dc, ch 1, shell in next shell, repeat from * all around ending to correspond, ch 1, join.
37th Rnd: Sl st to center of shell, ch 3, shell in same space, ** ch 3, sc in next loop, ch 3, sc in next loop, ch 3, shell in next shell, ch 1, skip 1 dc, dc in next dc, ch 2, skip 1 loop, sc in 1st loop of pineapple, * ch 3, sc in next loop, repeat from * 4 times, ch 2, dc in next dc, ch 1, shell in next shell, repeat from ** all around ending to correspond, ch 1, join.
38th Rnd: Sl st into shell, ch 3, shell in same space, * ch 4, sc in next loop, repeat from * twice, ch 4, shell in next shell, ch 3, skip next dc and next ch 2 loop, sc in 1st loop of pineapple, * ch 3, sc in next loop, repeat from * 3 times, ch 3, shell in next shell, repeat from 1st * all around ending to correspond, ch 3, join.
39th and 40th Rnds: Same as last round but having 1 more ch 4 loop between shells and 1 less ch 3 loop across each pineapple in each round.
41st Rnd: Same as last round but having 1 more loop in each section and 5 chs in each loop between shells and 1 less loop across each pineapple.
42nd Rnd: Sl st to center of shell, ch 3, shell in same space, * ch 5, sc in next loop, repeat from * 6 times, ch 5, shell in next shell, ch 3, sc in remaining loop of pineapple, ch 3, shell in next shell, repeat from 1st * all around ending to correspond, ch 3, join.
43rd Rnd: Sl st to center of shell, ch 3, shell in same space, * ch 5, sc in next loop, repeat from * 7 times, ch 5, shell in next shell, shell in next shell, repeat from 1st * all around ending to correspond, join.
44th Rnd: Sl st to center of next shell, * ch 6, sc in next loop, repeat from * 3 times, ch 1, trc in center st of next loop, * ch 4, sl st in top of trc for picot, trc in same space, repeat from * 4 times, ch 1, sc in next loop, * ch 6, sc in next loop, repeat from * twice, ch 6, sc in center of next shell, ch 4, sc in last sc for picot, sc in center of next shell, repeat from 1st * all around ending to correspond, join, cut thread.
