Brocade Doily Pattern

MATERIALS: J. & P. COATS "BIG BALL" BEST SIX CORD MERCERIZED CROCHET, Art. A.105,
Size 20: 1 ball of No. 1 White or No. 61 Ecru; or
CLARK'S "BIG BALL" MERCERIZED CROCHET,
Art. B.34, Size 20: 1 ball of No. 1 White, No. 61 Ecru or No. 42 Cream;
or
CLARK'S "BIG BALL" MERCERIZED CROCHET,
Art. B.345, Size 20:1 ball of No. 1 White or No. 61 Ecru.
Milwards Steel Crochet Hook No. 10.
Starting at center: Ch 12. Join with sl st to form ring.
1st rnd: Ch 4, holding back on hook the last loop of each tr, make 2 tr in ring, thread over and draw through all loops on hook - starting cluster made; (ch 2, holding back on hook the last loop of each tr, make 3 tr in ring, thread over and draw through all loops on hook-cluster made) 11 times; ch 2. Join with sl st to tip of starting cluster.
2nd rnd: Ch 8, * dc in tip of next cluster, ch 5. Repeat from * around. Join to 4th ch of ch-8.
3rd rnd: Make a starting cluster in joining, ch 3, dc in next sp, ch 3, * cluster in next dc, ch 3, dc in next sp, ch 3. Repeat from * around. Join to tip of starting cluster.
4th rnd: Make a starting cluster in joining, * ch 9, cluster in tip of next cluster. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 9. Join as before.
5th rnd: Starting cluster in joining, * ch 3, dc in next loop, ch 3, cluster in tip of next cluster. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 3. Join
6th rnd: In joining make starting cluster ch 5, and cluster; * in next dc make cluster, ch 5 and cluster; in tip of next cluster make cluster, ch 5 and cluster Repeat from * around, making cluster, ch 5 and cluster in last dc. Join.
7th rnd: Starting cluster in joining, * ch 5, sc in next ch-5 loop, ch 5, skip tip of next cluster, cluster in tip of next cluster. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 5. Join.
8th rnd: Starting cluster in joining, * ch 9, cluster in tip of next cluster. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 9. Join.
9th rnd: Starting cluster in joining, * ch 4, dc in next loop, ch 4, cluster in tip of next cluster. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 4, dc in last loop, ch 4. Join.
10th rnd: Starting cluster in joining, * ch 5, dc in next dc, ch 5, cluster in tip of next cluster. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 5, dc in last dc, ch 5. Join.
11th rnd: Starting cluster in joining, * ch 12, cluster in tip of next cluster. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 12. Join.
12th rnd: Starting cluster in joining, * ch 5, sc in next loop, ch 5, cluster in tip of next cluster. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 5, sc in last loop, ch 5. Join.
13th rnd: In joining make starting cluster, ch 5, and cluster; * in next sc make cluster, ch 5 and cluster; in tip of next cluster make cluster, ch 5 and cluster. Repeat from * around, making cluster, ch 5 and cluster in last sc. Join.
14th rnd: Starting cluster in joining, * ch 3, dc in next loop, ch 3, skip next cluster, cluster in tip of next cluster. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 3, dc in last loop, ch 3. Join.
15th rnd: Starting cluster in joining, cluster in tip of next cluster, * ch 9, dc in next dc, ch 9, (cluster in tip of next cluster) twice. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 9, dc in last dc, ch 9. Join.
16th rnd: Starting cluster in joining, * (ch 5, sc in next loop) twice; ch 5, skip next cluster, cluster in tip of next cluster. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 5. Join.
17th rnd: In joining make starting cluster, ch 5 and cluster; * ch 4, skip next loop, dc in next loop, ch 4, in tip of next cluster make cluster, ch 5 and cluster. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 5. Join.
18th rnd: In joining make starting cluster, ch 5 and cluster; in tip of next cluster make cluster, ch 5 and cluster; * ch 2, dc in next dc, ch 2, (in tip of next cluster make cluster, ch 5 and cluster) twice. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 2. Join.
19th rnd: In joining make starting cluster, ch 5 and cluster; * skip next cluster, (in tip of next cluster make cluster, ch 5 and cluster) 3 times. Repeat from * around, making cluster, ch 5 and cluster in tip of each of last 2 clusters. Join.
20th rnd: In joining make starting cluster, ch 5 and cluster; * skip next cluster, in tip of next cluster make cluster, ch 5 and cluster. Repeat from * around. Join.
21st rnd: Starting cluster in joining, * ch 11, skip next loop and following cluster, cluster in tip of next cluster, skip next cluster, in tip of next cluster make cluster, ch 7 and cluster; skip next cluster, cluster in tip of next cluster. Repeat from * around. Join.
22nd rnd: Starting cluster in joining, * ch 5, dc in next loop, ch 5, skip next cluster, cluster in tip of next cluster. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 5. Join.
23rd rnd: Starting cluster in joining, * ch 7, dc in next dc, ch 7, cluster in tip of next cluster, in next dc make tr, ch 5 and tr; cluster in tip of next cluster. Repeat from * around, making tr, ch 5 and tr in last dc. Join.
24th rnd: Starting cluster in joining, * ch 21, cluster in tip of next cluster, ch 1, cluster in tip of next cluster. Repeat from * around, ending with cluster in tip of last cluster, h dc in tip of first cluster to form last sp.
25th rnd: Starting cluster in sp just formed, * ch 5, in next loop make (cluster, ch 5) 4 times; cluster in next ch-1 sp between the 2 clusters. Repeat from * around, ending with ch 5. Join.
Break off and fasten. Starch lightly and press.
Doily measures 15 inches in diameter.
